CI Financial reports assets under management

TORONTO (June 1, 2017) – CI Financial Corp. (“CI”) today reported preliminary assets under management at May 31, 2017 of $123.3 billion and total assets of $164.0 billion.  

Assets under management grew by $12.7 billion or 11.5% year over year, and increased slightly in the month of May. Total assets gained $17.8 billion or 12.2% for the 12-month period and were up 0.2% in May.  

CI’s average assets under management for the quarter-to-date were $122.5 billion, up $3.1 billion or 2.6% from the first quarter of 2017. Average assets under management for the year-to-date were $120.7 billion, a gain of $9.8 billion or 8.8% from the average for fiscal year 2016. 

CI reported assets under advisement at Assante Wealth Management (Canada) Ltd. and Stonegate Private Counsel of $40.7 billion at May 31, 2017, representing an increase of $5.1 billion or 14.3% year over year.

Additional information about CI’s assets and financial position can be found below in the tables of statistics and on its website, www.cifinancial.com, in the Statistics section. These are the only statistics authorized by CI and CI takes no responsibility for reporting by any external sources.

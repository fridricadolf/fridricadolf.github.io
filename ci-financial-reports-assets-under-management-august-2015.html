CI Financial reports assets under management

TORONTO (September 2, 2015) – CI Financial Corp. (“CI”) today reported assets under management at August 31, 2015 of $107.4 billion and total assets of $140.9 billion.  

Assets under management grew by $5.2 billion or 5.1% year over year but declined by $3.7 billion or 3.3% in the month of August, reflecting recent volatility in global equity markets. Total assets gained $7.0 billion or 5.3% for the 12-month period but declined $4.9 billion or 3.3% in August. In comparison, the S&P/TSX Composite Index declined 8.7% over the 12-month period and fell 4.0% in the month of August. 

Average assets under management for the quarter-to-date as of August 31, 2015 were $109.7 billion, a decrease of 0.1% over the average for the second quarter. Average assets under management for the year-to-date were $108.5 billion, an increase of $10.1 billion or 10.3% over the average for fiscal 2014. 

CI also reported assets under advisement at Assante Wealth Management (Canada) Ltd. and Stonegate Private Counsel of $33.5 billion at August 31, 2015, an increase of 5.8% year over year.

Additional information about CI’s assets and financial position can be found below in the tables of preliminary statistics and on its website, www.cifinancial.com, in the Statistics section.

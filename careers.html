Why work for us?

 

Our People

Hailing from diverse backgrounds, our employees bring a wide range of experience and skill sets to CI. Some are new to the financial services industry, while others have built their careers around it.

Life@CI

The culture at CI Financial is a product of an integrated and communicative environment that supports the employee experience personally and professionally.

CI's administrative office space was designed with our employees in mind. A friendly game of pool or ping pong, catching the latest sporting event on one of our big screen TVs, or enjoying some time on our unique rooftop patio to take in the Lake Ontario view are all every day happenings here. Our main Toronto office is conveniently located steps from Toronto's Union Station.

We have a variety of fun events to give employees a chance to interact socially with colleagues across the company, to get to know each other better such as annual Summer and Holiday Parties, Women's Mentoring Program Networking events and various charitable causes.

Total Rewards

Hailing from diverse backgrounds, our employees bring a wide range of experience and skill sets to CI. Some are new to the financial services industry, while others have built their careers around it.

Benefits for full time employees:

  • Extended health care, including prescription drugs, vision care and professional services such as naturopathy and acupuncture
  • Dental care
  • Life Insurance, accidental death and dismemberment, Short and long-term disability and Optional life and spousal life insurance

Strike the perfect work-life balance:

  • Employee Savings Program – contribute up to 5% and we'll match contributions dollar-for-dollar to a maximum of $7,500 a year.
  • Training Reimbursement – expand your horizons and continue learning and Professional Designation Recognition Awards
  • Employee Assistance Program – personal, legal and financial counseling
  • Corporate Discounts – fitness memberships, home and auto insurance and rentals, and more
  • Health & Wellness Program – a range of events that provide a holistic approach to the health & wellness of our employees
  • Parental leave top-up
  • Ray Days – paid time off to volunteer for the registered charity of your choice

Is CI Right For You?

When it comes to hiring, the right fit is essential. CI employees must be comfortable in a fast-paced environment in an industry that's always changing. A healthy dose of ambition, a willingness to go the extra mile and a strong sense of camaraderie are what we look for in our team members.

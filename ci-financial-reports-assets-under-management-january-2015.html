CI Financial reports assets under management

TORONTO (February 3, 2015) – CI Financial Corp. (“CI”) today reported assets under management at January 31, 2015 of $105.5 billion, and total assets of $135.9 billion. Both represent month-end records for CI.

Assets under management grew by $13.7 billion or 14.9% year over year and were up $2.6 billion or 2.5% in the month of January. Total assets gained $16.9 billion or 14.2% for the year and $3.3 billion or 2.5% in January. In comparison, the S&P/TSX Composite Index was up 10.3% for the year and 0.5% in the month of January. 

At $105.5 billion, assets under management at January 31, 2015 were up $4.4 billion or 4.3% from the average assets under management for the fourth quarter of 2014, and were $7.1 billion or 7.2% higher than the average for fiscal 2014. 

CI also reported assets under administration at Assante Wealth Management (Canada) Ltd. of $30.4 billion at January 31, 2015, an increase of 11.9% year over year.

CI is scheduled to release its financial results for the quarter and year ending December 31, 2014 on Thursday, February 12, 2015 on CNW Group and www.cifinancial.com.

Additional information about CI’s assets and financial position can be found below in the tables of preliminary statistics and on its website, www.cifinancial.com, in the Statistics section.

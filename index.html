CI Financial Corp. is an independent Canadian company offering global asset management and wealth management advisory services. Since 1965, we have been driven by a commitment to provide investors and institutions with the highest-quality investments and advice. Today, our company has been entrusted with managing over $166 billion for our clients with approximately $125 billion in assets under management and more than $41 billion in assets under advisement (as at December 31, 2018).
